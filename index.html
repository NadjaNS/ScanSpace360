ScanSpace 360, we scan your business or private space for easier and better arrangement. 3D scanning interiors is the process of digitally capturing the dimensions and details of a physical space or object. It involves the use of a laser scanner or other 3D scanning technology to capture a highly detailed 3D representation of the interior space. The 3D scan data can then be used to create a 3D model, visualisation, or animation of the object or space.
